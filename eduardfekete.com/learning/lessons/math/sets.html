Introduction to Set Theory

Sets are the fundamental building blocks of mathematics. They help us understand data structures, logic, and the relationship between different groups of information.

Notation

We usually denote a set with a capital letter, like A.

An element x in a set A is written as: x∈A

Example

Let S be the set of even numbers less than 10: S={2,4,6,8}

Math Expressions (LaTeX)

The union of two sets A and B: A∪B={x∣x∈A or x∈B}

The intersection: A∩B={x∣x∈A and x∈B}

Exercise

What is the result of the intersection {1, 2} ∩ {2, 3}?
